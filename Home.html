Welcome to the Unruly Kingdom

Welcome to the Unruly Kindom where UnrulySalami reigns supreme!
The tyrant King, the bratwurst, was the ruler of the land. He piggybacked off of the backs of the hotdogs, hamburgers and salamis his entire life and rose to power. All of the lowly other finger foods were used as slaves to the king and his High Court of other brats, but there was one unruly salami who changed everything...

The uprising was swift and brutal. After an epic battle, UnrulySalami cast the tyrant King onto the grill where the titans of the land devoured the tyrant. UnrulySalami gave the King's high court the boot of justice, to live as Unruly did on the face-level part of the fridge, subject to the whims of the titans. All is chaotic after the uprising, but the law of the land is now Just and things are better than ever before.


In all seriousness...

UnrulySalami, aka Brad Pearce, is an Xbox gamer and Streamer, who started streaming while playing Call of Duty. During this time, Brad will be spending at least 4 hours a day studying and writing code while gaming in the evenings/at night.
